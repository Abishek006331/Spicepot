01 — Real-Time Business Scenario

The Challenge
at Hand

SpicePot operates 25 outlets across South India with dine-in, takeaway, and home delivery. Three critical pain points threaten growth and customer satisfaction.

01
⏳
Weekend Peak Overload
Weekend peak hours are generating unacceptably long waiting times, hurting the dine-in experience and leading to customer walk-outs.
02
📉
Menu Underperformers
Certain menu items are consistently underperforming across outlets, causing ingredient waste, higher costs, and reduced table revenue.
03
🚴
Delivery Quality Gap
Rising customer complaints cite cold food and excessive delivery times, eroding ratings on delivery platforms and damaging brand loyalty.

04 — Implementation Roadmap

The Analytics
Journey

Phase 01 — Data Collection
POS & Delivery Platform Integration
Connect all 25 outlet POS systems and delivery platform APIs into the IBM Cognos data warehouse for unified data ingestion.
Phase 02 — Reporting
Automated Revenue Reports
Schedule and distribute outlet-level and chain-wide sales reports to operations managers and the C-suite every morning.
Phase 03 — Exploration
Menu Performance Deep Dive
Use Cognos Exploration to identify the 30% of menu items driving 70% of revenue, and flag consistent underperformers for removal or rework.
Phase 04 — Dashboard & Stories
Live Command Centre + Narratives
Deploy the operations dashboard for real-time monitoring and use Story Making to communicate findings to stakeholders without data fluency.
